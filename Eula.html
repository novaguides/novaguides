Eula Lawrence


Eula Lawrence is a playable Cryo character in Genshin Impact.

Although a descendant of the infamous and tyrannical Lawrence Clan, Eula severed her ties with the clan and became the captain of the Reconnaissance Company with the Knights of Favonius.

Glacial Illumination

Brandishes her greatsword, dealing Cryo DMG to nearby opponents
and creating a Lightfall Sword that follows her around for a duration
of up to 7s.While present, the Lightfall Sword increases Eula's resistance
to interruption.

When Eula's own Normal Attack, Elemental Skill, and Elemental Burst deal DMG
to opponents, they will charge the Lightfall Sword, which can gain an energy
stack once every 0.1s.

Once its duration ends, the Lightfall Sword will descend and explode violently,
dealing Physical DMG to nearby opponents. This DMG scales on the number
of energy stacks the Lightfall Sword has accumulated. If Eula leaves the
field, the Lightfall Sword will immediately explode.

Weapons
Song of Broken Pines

Eula's signature weapon, offering high base ATK, Physical DMG% substat, and ATK%. It's perfect for Eula and enhances her combos.
Wolf's Gravestone

A versatile weapon with a teamwide ATK% buff. While it can outdamage Song of Broken Pines under certain conditions, it's harder to proc its passive with Eula's Burst.
The Unforged

Similar to Wolf's Gravestone but weaker. It provides ATK buffs and requires a shield for maximum power.
Skyward Pride

Its power depends on your Energy Recharge needs. Eula can utilize its passive well, but it's situational and may be outperformed by some 4-star options.
Redhorn Stonethresher

Offers high CRIT DMG% but suffers from poor base ATK and passive. It's best when used with Bennett to overcome its limitations.
Serpent's Spine

A powerful weapon with high CRIT Rate substats and a universal DMG% buff.
Luxurious Sea-Lord

The best F2P option for Eula with a 24% DMG buff for her Burst. It's cost-effective and highly recommended for F2P players.
Akoumaru

An unstable weapon that's situational. It can be great when used with Raiden and has high refinements, but it depends on Energy cost in your team.
Snow-Tombed Starsilver

A competitive F2P option, although not as favored as Luxurious Sea-Lord. It's straightforward but has a slight inconsistency.
Prototype Archaic

Similar in power to Snow-Tombed Starsilver but offers higher consistency.
Blackcliff Slasher

Powerful at 3 stacks but rare to achieve. Good at lower stacks but not worth trading 25 Starglitters for.

Summary
Role

Eula is primarily a Physical Damage dealer, making her suitable for the Main DPS role. Her damage output relies on her Normal Attacks and Elemental Burst.
Weapon Choice

Opt for a claymore weapon that boosts Physical Damage or Critical Rate, such as the "Serpent Spine" or "The Unforged." These weapons enhance her damage potential. Her best in slot is her signature weapon, "Song of Broken Pines"
Artifact Sets

The best artifact set for Eula is the "Pale Flame" set, which increases her Physical Damage and provides a bonus when using her Elemental Burst. You can also consider the "Bloodstained Chivalry" set for increased Physical Damage.
Main Stats

Prioritize artifacts with ATK% and Physical Damage Bonus as the main stats. For the Sands, Goblet, and Circlet, aim for ATK% or Crit DMG% as the main stats, depending on her substats.
Substats

Look for substats that boost Crit Rate, Crit Damage, ATK%, and Energy Recharge. These stats will significantly improve Eula's damage output.
Talents

Level up her Normal Attack (Favonius Bladework - Edel), Elemental Burst (Glacial Illumination), and Elemental Skill (Icetide Vortex) talents to maximize her damage potential.
Team Comps

Consider pairing Eula with characters who can apply Cryo status effects to trigger Superconduct and reduce enemy Physical RES. Characters like Qiqi, Diona, or Rosaria can be great support options.
