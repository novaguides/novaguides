Welcome Traveler!

Nova Guides is a website dedicated to providing travelers all over the world with guides for different builds in the open world role-playing game, Genshin Impact. Builds refer to “the arrangement of skills, items, and other attributes that make a character” and this site has all you need for builds based on characters, weapons, and even artifact sets.
