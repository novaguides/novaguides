ARTIFACTS

Artifacts are items in Genshin Impact that can be equipped on Characters to increase their Stats. It is the second tab in the Inventory. There are 5 types of Artifacts that can be equipped: Flower of Life, Plume of Death, Sands of Eon, Goblet of Eonothem, and. Circlet of Logos.

ADVENTURER

2-Piece Bonus

4-Piece Bonus

Max HP increased by 1,000

Opening chest regenerates 30% Max HP over 5s

ARCHAIC PETRA

2-Piece Bonus

4-Piece Bonus

Gain a 15% Geo DMG Bonus.

Upon obtaining an Elemental Shard created through a Crystallize Reaction,
all party members gain a 35% DMG Bonus for that particular element for 10s. Only
one form of Elemental DMG Bonus can be gained in this manner at any one time.

BERSERKER

2-Piece Bonus

4-Piece Bonus

CRIT Rate +12%

When HP is below 70%, CRIT Rate increases by an additional 24%.
